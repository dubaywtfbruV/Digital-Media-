Dogs

The different types of breeds

  1. Borzoi
      2. A Russian Hunting Sighthound. It was formerly used for wolf hunting. They live for around 7 to 10 years. They are known to be intelligent, gentle, respectful, independent, athletic, and quiet.
  2. Husky
      3. A dog used in the polar regions, primarily for sledding. They live for around 10 to 15 years. They are known for being playful, funloving and physical.
  3. Corgi
      4. This is a small type of herding dog that originated in Wales. The world Corgi comes from the welsh words cor and ci, "dwarf" and "dog". They live for around 12 to 15 years.
  4. Pomeranian
      5. Originating from north-west Poland and north-east Germany. They're classed as a toy dog because of their small size. They're expected to live for 12 to 16 years. They're known for being playful, extroverted, friendly, and active.
